30 Hour Fast 2011

I consider myself luck that over the past 3 years I’ve been able to be a part of Parker Hill and their student ministries department. Over this last weekend I was able to take part in what is called the 30 hour fast. Basically all of the students from middle school to high school raise money and awareness by giving up their right to eat for 30 hours.
